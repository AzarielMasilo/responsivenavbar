Saturday Market

January 7th

On Saturday, Dilara took us to the market.

It is a wonderfully electric mix of goods from all over the world - delightfully random. I dusted off my bargaining skills to buy a metal bracelet with camels on it and Maggie bought a lapiz lazuli necklace from Afganistan.

We took a catamaran from the marina to Failaka island where we got to explore a bit, relax on the bitch and go swimming. The water in the gulf is incredibly still.

That night i hungout in a cafe with two freinds, drinking traditional lemonade with mint. It's delicious; maybe I should send in a flavor suggestion to Morano Gelato?
